# Create a saved search

In Knokd, you can create and save searches for yourself and for your clients. Both start in the same place.

## Creating searches

1. [Login](https://www.knokd.com/login) to Knokd.
2. Go to "Searches" in the left navigation.
3. Click “Create new search”
4. Enter the criteria for your search. 
5. If the search is for yourself, you're done.
6. It it’s for a follower or a client, then click on the search you just created.
7. See "Assign follower" in the right hand corner.
8. Toggle "Assign search to a follow" on.
9. Select the follower you'd like to assign the search to. You will need to create the follower if you haven't already.
10. Click "Save search".

## Editing searches

Directly editing search is not currently supported in Knokd. To change your search:

1. [Login](https://www.knokd.com/login) to Knokd.
2. Click on "Searches" in the left navigation.
3. Click the search you need to edit.
4. Click "Edit search" button near the tittle.
5. Make your edit.
6. Click "Save Search"
