# Saved Searches

Searches are saved in Knokd to help you and your contacts filter through listings based on an individual's buying criteria. In Knokd, you can [create searches](#create-a-search) for yourself and/or [assign the saved search to your contacts](#assign-a-search-to-a-contact).

## Create a search

1. <a href="https://app.knokd.ca/login" target="_blank">Log in to Knokd</a>.
2. Click **Searches** in the sidebar menu.
3. Click **Create new search**. 
4. *(optional)* If you like, [assign this search to one or more contacts](#assign-a-search-to-a-contact).
4. Enter the criteria for your search. 
5. Click **Save Search**.

## Edit a search

To change your search:

1. <a href="https://app.knokd.ca/login" target="_blank">Log in to Knokd</a>.
2. Click **Searches** in the sidebar menu.
3. Click the search you want to edit.
4. Click **Edit search** near the title.
5. Make your changes and click **Save Search**.

## Assign a search to a contact:

While [creating](#create-a-search) or [editing a search](#edit-a-search), you may choose to [assign the search](/saved-searches/create-a-saved-search#assign-a-search) to a contact so that they receive updates about listings that meet their criteria.

To assign a saved search:

1. <a href="https://app.knokd.ca/login" target="_blank">Log in to Knokd</a>.
1. Click **Searches** in the sidebar menu.
1. Click a Search Name to view its results.
1. Click **Edit search**. 
1. In the **Assign contact** card, toggle-on **Assign search to a contact**.
1. From the drop-down menu that appears, select the contact.
<Note>If the contact does not appear in the drop-down list, make sure you first [add the contact](/contacts/add-contacts) to Knokd.</Note>
1. Click **Save Search**.
