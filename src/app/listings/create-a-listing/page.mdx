# Create a listing

All Knokd users can add unlimited listings. To add a listing:

1. <a href="https://app.knokd.ca/login" target="_blank">Log in</a> to Knokd.
1. Click **Listings** in the sidebar menu.
1. In the sub-menu that opens, click **My Listings**.
1. Click **Create listing** in the header.
1. Enter in the details of your listing.
  <Note>Enable the **Assignment** toggle if the to-be owner wishes to sell the rights to the property. Permission from the builder is required to do this.</Note>
1. Click **Save Draft**.
1. If your listing does not include an MLS date then it is considered an exlusive listing. You will be asked to confirm that you have gotten written permission from you client to forgo MLS listing.
