# Create a listing

All Knokd users can add unlimited listings. To add a listing:

1. <a href="https://app.knokd.ca/login" target="_blank">Log in</a> to Knokd.
1. Click **Listings** in the sidebar menu to open a sub-menu.
1. Click **My listings**.
1. Click **Create listing** in the header.
1. Enter in the details of your listing.
  <Note>You should toggle-on the **Assignment** option if the seller wishes to sell the *rights* to the property. Permission from the builder is required to do this.</Note>
1. Click **Save Draft**.
1. If your listing does not include an MLS date then it is considered an exclusive listing. You will be asked to confirm that you have written permission from you client to abstain from listing on an MLS.
