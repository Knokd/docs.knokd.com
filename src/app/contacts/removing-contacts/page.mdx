# Removing Contacts

## As an agent

As an agent, you can remove any contact from your contact list.

1. Go to your **Contacts** page.
2. Select the contact you want to remove.
3. Click on **Edit contact**.
4. Click on **Delecte contact**.

Contact will be removed from your contact list. All their information will be deleted from our system.

## As a contact

As a contact, you can remove yourself from FirstList at any time.

1. Log in to FirstList.
2. Click on **Settings** in the sidebar navigation.
3. Find the **Remove account** button in the bottom left corner.
4. Click on **Remove account**.
5. Confirm your decision by clicking on **Yes, confirm**.

Your account will be deleted from our system. All your information will be deleted.
