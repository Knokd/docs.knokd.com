# Agents

A real-estate agent is a dynamic professional who manages client relationships, markets properties, and negotiates deals to close sales efficiently and effectively. Your team/brokerage can have several agents.

<Note>All agent accounts must be verified before accessing Knokd.</Note>

## Removing agents

As an admin, you can invite an agent to your team:

1. <a href="https://app.knokd.com/login" target="_blank">Log in to Knokd</a>.
1. Click **Settings** in the left hand navigation.
1. Click **Manage agents** under **Manage team** section.
1. Find a team member you want to remove.
1. Click **Remove**.
