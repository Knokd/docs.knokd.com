# Welcome to FirstList (formerly Knokd)

FirstList (formerly Knokd) gives realtors a new competitive edge for off-market listings. FirstList (formerly Knokd) is an off-market platform, built with [RCP compliance](/welcome-to-knokd/realtor-cooperation-policy), for agents - by agents.

<h2 className="flex gap-2 items-center"><HomeIcon className="h-8 w-8 fill-indigo-500/20 stroke-indigo-500 transition-colors group-hover/button:stroke-indigo-400" /> Off-market platform </h2>

FirstList (formerly Knokd) is an agent-only tool that lets you [share exclusive and coming soon listings](/listings/sharing-link). These may include residential, assignment, new build or multi-unit residential listings. 


<h2 className="flex gap-2 items-center"><ClipboardCheckIcon className="h-8 w-8 fill-indigo-500/20 stroke-indigo-500 transition-colors group-hover/button:stroke-indigo-400" /> Built for the RCP </h2>

We are built for the [Realtor Cooperation Policy (RCP)](/welcome-to-knokd/realtor-cooperation-policy). We help you categorise your listings and share them compliantly. This may be internally within your brokerage, [via 1–1 share](/listings/1-1-sharing), or across our network when permitted. 

<h2 className="flex gap-2 items-center"><UserCircleIcon className="h-8 w-8 fill-indigo-500/20 stroke-indigo-500 transition-colors group-hover/button:stroke-indigo-400" /> For agents, by agents</h2>

We believe that buying a home with an experienced agent leads to the best results for clients, and our mission is to provide you with tools to leverage your advantage over online portals.
