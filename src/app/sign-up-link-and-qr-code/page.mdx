# Sign up link & QR Code

All Knokd agent users are equipped with a personal Knokd landing page and QR Code. To access this feature:

1. <a href="https://app.knokd.com/login" target="_blank">Log in to Knokd</a>.
1. Click **Sign up link** in the sidebar menu to open a sub-menu.
1. Click **View link** or **Download** QR Code.
1. Click **Learn more** on each sharing method for more information.

We strive to provide simple and effective ways for agents to share non-MLS listings in an RCP compliant manner.
