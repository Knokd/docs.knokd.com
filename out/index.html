Welcome to Knokd

Knokd gives realtors a new competitive edge for off-market listings. Knokd is an off-market platform, built with RCP compliance, for agents - by agents.

Off-market platform

Knokd is an agent-only tool that lets you share exclusive and coming soon listings. These may include residential, assignment, new build or multi-unit residential listings.

Built for the RCP

We are built for the Realtor Cooperation Policy (RCP). We help you categorise your listings and share them compliantly. This may be internally within your brokerage, via 1–1 share, or across our network when permitted.

For agents, by agents

We believe that buying a home with an experienced agent leads to the best results for clients, and our mission is to provide you with tools to leverage your advantage over online portals.
